Okay so I'm going to share something that's been bugging me and I know it's going to upset the "always be positive on LinkedIn" crowd but here goes.

I've had three interviews this month where the company ghosted me after the final round. Not a rejection email. Not a "we went another direction." Just silence. One of them I'd done five rounds of interviews with, including a take-home that took me a weekend.

This is not okay. I don't care how busy your recruiting team is. A form email takes thirty seconds. I know because I used to manage a recruiting team and we sent them to every single candidate because it's basic human decency.

I get that hiring is hard. I get that things fall through cracks. But five rounds and a take-home and then nothing? That's not a crack. That's a canyon.

I'm not going to name the companies because that's not what this post is about. What it's about is this: if you're in a position to influence how your company treats candidates, please push for a closed loop. Every candidate gets a response. Every single one. Even if it's a no. Especially if it's a no.

Rant over. Back to applying to things and pretending I'm fine with the process.
